About Us

TOTAL PRIME ENGINEERING began in 2008 as a Sealing Elements company. Today, we are highly recognized in the market as a very reliable and trustworthy partner, offering quality products at very reasonable prices. We are also known as the largest stockist for sealing businesses in Indonesia. Since 2013, we have expanded into the Repair and Production of Hydraulic Cylinders.

Currently, we have a production facility spanning 5350 square meters in Cikarang, West Java, and another production facility in Balikpapan covering 1250 square meters. These two facilities enable us to efficiently undertake all processes, from design to repair and production, under one roof.
