About Us

PT. Kusuma Sarana Dedikasi (KSD) is a General Supplier and Trading company established on October 10, 2024, in Bekasi, West Java. We are engaged in trade and distribution for various sectors, including industry, construction, corporate offices, and HVAC.

With a vision to become a leading and trusted company, we are committed to being the solution for all your business needs. We provide high-quality products supported by fast, responsive, and professional service, making us a reliable 'one-stop solution' partner.
